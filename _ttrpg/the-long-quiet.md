A journalling game in second playtest. You play a single crew member of a generation ship that has lost its purpose; each session, a prompt deck pulls you between memory, duty, and the slow encroaching silence.

The full version will ship as a 64-page softcover. A free playtest packet is linked above.

**Status notes (May 2026):** core resolution is settling. The biggest remaining question is whether the ship itself should have its own voice. Probably yes.
